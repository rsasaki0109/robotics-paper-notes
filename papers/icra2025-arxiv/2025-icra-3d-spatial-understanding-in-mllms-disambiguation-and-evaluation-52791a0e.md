## TL;DR

- Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) have made significant progress in tasks such as image captioning and question answering.
- However, while these models can generate realistic captions, they often struggle with providing precise instructions, particularly when it comes to localizing and disambiguating objects in complex 3D environments.
- This capability is critical as MLLMs become more integrated with collaborative robotic systems.
