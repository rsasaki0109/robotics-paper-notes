## TL;DR

- Comprehensive and consistent dynamic scene understanding from camera input is essential for advanced autonomous systems.
- Traditional camera-based perception tasks like 3D object tracking and semantic occupancy prediction lack either spatial comprehensiveness or temporal consistency.
- In this work, we introduce a brand-new task, Camera-based 4D Panoptic Occupancy Tracking, which simultaneously addresses panoptic occupancy segmentation and object tracking from camera-only input.
