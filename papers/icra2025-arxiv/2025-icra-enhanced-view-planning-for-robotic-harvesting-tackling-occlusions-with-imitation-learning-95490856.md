## TL;DR

- In agricultural automation, inherent occlusion presents a major challenge for robotic harvesting.
- We propose an imitation learning-based viewpoint planning approach to actively adjust camera viewpoint and capture unobstructed images of the target crop.
- Traditional viewpoint planners and existing learning-based methods, depend on manually designed evaluation metrics or reward functions, often struggle to generalize to complex, unseen scenarios.
