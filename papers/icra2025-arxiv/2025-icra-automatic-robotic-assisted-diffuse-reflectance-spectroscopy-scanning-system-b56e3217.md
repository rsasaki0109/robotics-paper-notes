## TL;DR

- Diffuse Reflectance Spectroscopy (DRS) is a well-established optical technique for tissue composition assessment which has been validated for tumour detection to ensure the complete removal of cancerous tissue.
- While point-wise assessment has many potential applications, incorporating automated large-area scanning would enable holistic tissue sampling with higher consistency.
- We propose a robotic system to facilitate autonomous DRS scanning with hybrid visual servoing control.
