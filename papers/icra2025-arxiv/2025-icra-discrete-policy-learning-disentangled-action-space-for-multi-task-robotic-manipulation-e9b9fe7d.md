## TL;DR

- Learning visuomotor policy for multi-task robotic manipulation has been a long-standing challenge for the robotics community.
- The difficulty lies in the diversity of action space: typically, a goal can be accomplished in multiple ways, resulting in a multimodal action distribution for a single task.
- The complexity of action distribution escalates as the number of tasks increases.
