## TL;DR

- However, the inclusion of robotic teammates brings along new challenges in understanding teammate intent and communication.
- To address this limitation, we propose a new training review (TR) tool, called the Virtual Spectator Interface (VSI), to enhance human-robot team performance and situational awareness (SA) in a simulated search mission.
- The results of our experiments demonstrated that the VSI did not result in significantly better team performance than other conditions.
