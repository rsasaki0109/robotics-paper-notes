## TL;DR

- While there has been notable progress with two-fingered grippers, learning autonomous tasks without teleoperation remains a difficult problem for multi-fingered robot hands.
- In this work, we present HuDOR, a technique that enables online finetuning of the policy by constructing a reward function from the human video.
- Our experiments across four tasks, show that HuDOR outperforms alternatives with an average of 4x improvement.
