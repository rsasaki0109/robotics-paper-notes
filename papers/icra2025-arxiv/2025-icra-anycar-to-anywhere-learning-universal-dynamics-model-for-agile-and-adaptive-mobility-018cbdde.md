## TL;DR

- Recent works in the robot learning community have successfully introduced generalist models capable of controlling various robot embodiments across a wide range of tasks, such as navigation and locomotion.
- However, achieving agile control, which pushes the limits of robotic performance, still relies on specialist models that require extensive parameter tuning.
- To leverage generalist-model adaptability and flexibility while achieving specialist-level agility, we propose AnyCar, a transformer-based generalist dynamics model designed for agile control of various wheeled robots.
