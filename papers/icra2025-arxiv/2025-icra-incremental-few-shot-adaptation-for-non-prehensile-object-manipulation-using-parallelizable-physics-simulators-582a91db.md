## TL;DR

- Few-shot adaptation is an important capability for intelligent robots that perform tasks in open-world settings such as everyday environments or flexible production.
- In this paper, we propose a novel approach for non-prehensile manipulation which incrementally adapts a physics-based dynamics model for model-predictive control (MPC).
- The model prediction is aligned with a few examples of robot-object interactions collected with the MPC.
