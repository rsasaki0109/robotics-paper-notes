## TL;DR

- Recently, neural radiance fields (NeRF) have gained significant attention in the field of visual localization.
- However, existing NeRF-based approaches either lack geometric constraints or require extensive storage for feature matching, limiting their practical applications.
- To address these challenges, we propose an efficient and novel visual localization approach based on the neural implicit map with complementary features.
