## TL;DR

- 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) has become a popular solution in SLAM, as it can produce high-fidelity novel views.
- However, previous GS-based methods primarily target indoor scenes and rely on RGB-D sensors or pre-trained depth estimation models, hence underperforming in outdoor scenarios.
- To address this issue, we propose a RGB-only gaussian splatting SLAM method for unbounded outdoor scenesOpenGS-SLAM.
