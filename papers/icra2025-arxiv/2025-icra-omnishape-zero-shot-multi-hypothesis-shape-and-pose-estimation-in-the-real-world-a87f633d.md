## TL;DR

- We would like to estimate the pose and full shape of an object from a single observation, without assuming known 3D model or category.
- In this work, we propose OmniShape, the first method of its kind to enable probabilistic pose and shape estimation.
- OmniShape is based on the key insight that shape completion can be decoupled into two multi-modal distributions: one capturing how measurements project into a normalized object reference frame defined by the dataset and the other modelling a prior over object geometries represented as triplanar neural fields.
