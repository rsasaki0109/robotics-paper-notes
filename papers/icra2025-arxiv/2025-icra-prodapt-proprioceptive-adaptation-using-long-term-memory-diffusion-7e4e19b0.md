## TL;DR

- Diffusion models have revolutionized imitation learning, allowing robots to replicate complex behaviours.
- However, diffusion often relies on cameras and other exteroceptive sensors to observe the environment and lacks long-term memory.
- In space, military, and underwater applications, robots must be highly robust to failures in exteroceptive sensors, operating using only proprioceptive information.
