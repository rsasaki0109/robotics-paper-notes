## TL;DR

- Object 6D pose estimation is a critical challenge in robotics, particularly for manipulation tasks.
- While prior research combining visual and tactile (visuotactile) information has shown promise, these approaches often struggle with generalization due to the limited availability of visuotactile data.
- In this paper, we introduce ViTa-Zero, a zero-shot visuotactile pose estimation framework.
