- Surgical procedures are inherently complex and dynamic, with intricate dependencies and various execution paths.
- Accurate identification of the intentions behind critical actions, referred to as Primary Intentions (PIs), is crucial to understanding and planning the procedure.
- This paper presents a novel framework that advances PI recognition in instructional videos by combining top-down grammatical structure with bottom-up visual cues.
