## TL;DR

- A modern smart factory runs a manufacturing procedure using a collection of programmable machines.
- Typically, materials are ferried between these machines using a team of mobile robots.
- To embed a manufacturing procedure in a smart factory, a factory operator must a) assign its processes to the smart factory's machines and b) determine how agents should carry materials between machines.
