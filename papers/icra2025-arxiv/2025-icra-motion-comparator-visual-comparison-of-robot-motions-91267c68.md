## TL;DR

- In this paper, we follow a rigorous design process to create Motion Comparator, a web-based tool that facilitates the comprehension, comparison, and communication of robot motions.
- Roboticists compare robot motions for tasks such as parameter tuning, troubleshooting, and deciding between possible motions.
- To demonstrate the applications of Motion Comparator, we discuss four case studies in which our tool is used for motion selection, troubleshooting, parameter tuning, and motion review.
