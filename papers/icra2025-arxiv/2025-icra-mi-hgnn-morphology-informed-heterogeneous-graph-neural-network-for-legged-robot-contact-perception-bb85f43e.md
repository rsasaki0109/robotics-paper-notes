## TL;DR

- We present a Morphology-Informed Heterogeneous Graph Neural Network (MI-HGNN) for learning-based contact perception.
- The architecture and connectivity of the MI-HGNN are constructed from the robot morphology, in which nodes and edges are robot joints and links, respectively.
- By incorporating the morphology-informed constraints into a neural network, we improve a learning-based approach using model-based knowledge.
