## TL;DR

- Uncrewed Aerial Vehicle (UAV) research faces challenges with safety, scalability, costs, and ecological impact when conducting hardware testing.
- In this paper we present an overview of 44 UAV simulators, including in-depth, systematic comparisons for 14 of the simulators.
- High-fidelity simulators offer a vital solution by replicating real-world conditions to enable the development and evaluation of novel perception and control algorithms.
