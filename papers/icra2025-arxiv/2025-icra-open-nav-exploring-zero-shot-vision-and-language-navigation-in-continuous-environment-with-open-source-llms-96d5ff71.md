## TL;DR

- Vision-and-Language Navigation (VLN) tasks require an agent to follow textual instructions to navigate through 3D environments.
- Traditional approaches use supervised learning methods, relying heavily on domain-specific datasets to train VLN models.
- Recent methods try to utilize closed-source large language models (LLMs) like GPT-4 to solve VLN tasks in zero-shot manners, but face challenges related to expensive token costs and potential data breaches in real-world applications.
