## TL;DR

- Current LiDAR-based Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) multi-agent perception systems have shown the significant success on 3D object detection.
- While these models perform well in the trained clean weather, they struggle in unseen adverse weather conditions with the domain gap.
- In this paper, we propose a Domain Generalization based approach, named V2X-DGW, for LiDAR-based 3D object detection on multi-agent perception system under adverse weather conditions.
