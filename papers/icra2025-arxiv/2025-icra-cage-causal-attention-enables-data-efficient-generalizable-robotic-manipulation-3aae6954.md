## TL;DR

- Generalization in robotic manipulation remains a critical challenge, particularly when scaling to new environments with limited demonstrations.
- This paper introduces CAGE, a novel robotic manipulation policy designed to overcome these generalization barriers by integrating the pre-trained visual representation with causal attention mechanism.
- CAGE utilizes the powerful feature extraction capabilities of the vision foundation model DINOv2, combined with LoRA fine-tuning for robust environment understanding.
