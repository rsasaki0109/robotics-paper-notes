## TL;DR

- In this work, we aim to learn a unified vision-based policy for multi-fingered robot hands to manipulate a variety of objects in diverse poses.
- Though prior work has shown benefits of using human videos for policy learning, performance gains have been limited by the noise in estimated trajectories.
- Moreover, reliance on privileged object information such as ground-truth object states further limits the applicability in realistic scenarios.
