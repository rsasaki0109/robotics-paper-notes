## TL;DR

- This work studies the planning problem for robotic systems under both quantifiable and unquantifiable uncertainty.
- The objective is to enable the robotic systems to optimally fulfill high-level tasks specified by Linear Temporal Logic (LTL) formulas.
- To capture both types of uncertainty in a unified modelling framework, we utilise Markov Decision Processes with Set-valued Transitions (MDPSTs).
