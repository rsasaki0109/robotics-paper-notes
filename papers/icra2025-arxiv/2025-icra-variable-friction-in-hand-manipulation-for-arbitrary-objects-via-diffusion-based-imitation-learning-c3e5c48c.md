## TL;DR

- Dexterous in-hand manipulation (IHM) for arbitrary objects is challenging due to the rich and subtle contact process.
- Variable-friction manipulation is an alternative approach to dexterity, previously demonstrating robust and versatile 2D IHM capabilities with only two single-joint fingers.
- However, the hard-coded manipulation methods for variable friction hands are restricted to regular polygon objects and limited target poses, as well as requiring the policy to be tailored for each object.
