## TL;DR

- Multimodal task specification is essential for enhanced robotic performance, where Cross-modality Alignment enables the robot to holistically understand complex task instructions.
- Directly annotating multimodal instructions for model training proves impractical, due to the sparsity of paired multimodal data.
- In this study, we demonstrate that by leveraging unimodal instructions abundant in real data, we can effectively teach robots to learn multimodal task specifications.
