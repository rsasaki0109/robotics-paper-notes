## TL;DR

- Leveraging the powerful reasoning capabilities of large language models (LLMs), recent LLM-based robot task planning methods yield promising results.
- However, they mainly focus on single or multiple homogeneous robots on simple tasks.
- Practically, complex long-horizon tasks always require collaboration among multiple heterogeneous robots especially with more complex action spaces, which makes these tasks more challenging.
