## TL;DR

- Low-cost, vision-centric 3D perception systems for autonomous driving have made significant progress in recent years, narrowing the gap to expensive LiDAR-based methods.
- The primary challenge in becoming a fully reliable alternative lies in robust depth prediction capabilities, as camera-based systems struggle with long detection ranges and adverse lighting and weather conditions.
- In this work, we introduce HyDRa, a novel camera-radar fusion architecture for diverse 3D perception tasks.
