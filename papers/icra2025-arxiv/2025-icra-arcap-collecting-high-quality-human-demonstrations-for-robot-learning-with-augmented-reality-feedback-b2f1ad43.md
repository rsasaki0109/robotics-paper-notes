## TL;DR

- Recent progress in imitation learning from human demonstrations has shown promising results in teaching robots manipulation skills.
- To further scale up training datasets, recent works start to use portable data collection devices without the need for physical robot hardware.
- However, due to the absence of on-robot feedback during data collection, the data quality depends heavily on user expertise, and many devices are limited to specific robot embodiments.
