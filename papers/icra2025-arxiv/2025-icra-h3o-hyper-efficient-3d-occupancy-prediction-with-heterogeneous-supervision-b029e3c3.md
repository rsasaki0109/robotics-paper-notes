## TL;DR

- 3D occupancy prediction has recently emerged as a new paradigm for holistic 3D scene understanding and provides valuable information for downstream planning in autonomous driving.
- Most existing methods, however, are computationally expensive, requiring costly attention-based 2D-3D transformation and 3D feature processing.
- In this paper, we present a novel 3D occupancy prediction approach, named H3O, which features highly efficient architecture designs and incurs a significantly lower computational cost as compared to the current state-of-the-art methods.
