## TL;DR

- Learning-based methods, such as imitation learning (IL) and reinforcement learning (RL), can produce excel control policies over challenging agile robot tasks, such as sports robot.
- However, no existing work has harmonized learning-based policy with model-based methods to reduce training complexity and ensure the safety and stability for agile badminton robot control.
- In this paper, we introduce Hamlet, a novel hybrid control system for agile badminton robots.
