## TL;DR

- Tactile sensing and the manipulation of delicate objects are critical challenges in robotics.
- This study presents a vision-based magnetic-actuated whisker array sensor that integrates these functions.
- The sensor features eight whiskers arranged circularly, supported by an elastomer membrane and actuated by electromagnets and permanent magnets.
